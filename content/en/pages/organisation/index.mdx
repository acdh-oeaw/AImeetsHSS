The conference is jointly organised by five institutes (Humanities & Socal Sciences) of the Austrian Academy of Sciences

[Austrian Centre for Digital Humanities and Cultural Heritage ](https://www.oeaw.ac.at/acdh/)(ACDH-CH)

[Austrian Archaeological Institute ](https://www.oeaw.ac.at/en/oeai/)(OeAI)

[Institute for Comparative Media and Communication Studies ](https://www.oeaw.ac.at/cmc/)(CMC)

[Institute for Medieval Research ](https://www.oeaw.ac.at/en/imafo/home)(IMAFO)

[Vienna Institute of Demography ](https://www.oeaw.ac.at/vid)(VID)

in cooperation with [MLA2S – Machine Learning at the Austrian Academy of Sciences](https://www.oeaw.ac.at/mla2s/), a topical platform of the Austrian Academy of Sciences.
