The legal basis of processing your data is your registration for, your participation in and/or the performance of the Conference AI meets HSS, the compliance with legal obligations (e.g. accounting obligations) or a legitimate interest (e.g. publication of speakers and their contribution, contact with participants, documentation of the conference) of OeAW (Art 6 (1) b, c, f GDPR). Your data will be processed for the administration of the conference, to provide you with information about (e.g. changes) and documents of the event, to reach you for subsequent discussions/questions as well as for the documentation of the conference.

Your data will be processed on OeAW infrastructure.

You have the right to request access to and rectification or erasure of personal data or restriction of processing or to object to processing as well as the right to data portability. Furthermore, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the competent supervisory authority in Austria or another EU member state. The Austrian supervisory authority is the [Austrian Data Protection Authority](https://www.dsb.gv.at/).
